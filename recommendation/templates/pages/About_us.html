Recommender

A Recommendation System for helping students to choose the right college courses with an efficient and modern way.
we are a group of seniors and we dedicated our graduation project to solving the main problem that irritated us the most at the begging of each semester which was choosing the suitable courses that would let us graduate with the highest GPA and the lowest number of semesters .
We faced these Problems when we enrolled each semester so we are pleased to make a similar recommendion system that is designed based on the ratings given by a student to a few courses, the course recommender application will predict a user's preferences and will further recommend courses that a student might want to enroll in.
we built this system to recommend the best for you based on the collected data from previous students and their enrollments.
